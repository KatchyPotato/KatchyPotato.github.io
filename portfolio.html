About Me

My name is Judah Gaudy, otherwise known as Katchy Potato online. The name Katchy Potato came to me when trying to pick a username for my Scratch account, and has stuck with me ever since. The profile picture above is my online self, a humanoid ninja potato. My first ever experience with coding was on Scratch, an online learning platform for kids. Scratch teaches logic flow, a very important part of the development process, with block coding. I created on scratch for three years, completing and sharing over 50 projects. On May 15, 2025, Scratch decided to feature a project of mine, Pixel Playground, on their front page. in the 2025-2026 school year, I decided to join the Software and App Design course at East Valley Institute of Technology [EVIT]. I passed the certification tests for multiple languages, got accepted into the National Technical Honors Society [NTHS], and even built this website! I am continuing to learn and progress in my journey as a developer. Outside of coding I enjoy modeling with paper, playing basketball, tinkering with my Arduino, relaxing with puzzle-platformer games, creating pixel art, and nerding out about Zelda. I'm excited to see where this career takes me! -Judah Gaudy
